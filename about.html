About us


N-Crypt was born out of the idea of a platform that lets users control the information flow.

We allow users an easy way to navigate our extensive settings to control exactly what they share and with whom. Our settings are never hidden away and feature prominent on your feed and profile."

N-Crypt provides three layers of connection to allow users to fully customize who gets access to what.

Layer One: Friends

This is the layer closest to you. Users who are friends with each other have full access to your profile, name and feed. This makes sure that only the people closest to you can follow your online activities.

Layer Two: Network

Users in this group will not be able to see your profile picture, they have limited access to your profile information and will not be able to see posts only intended for friends. They have will be able to see your full profile name.

Layer Three: Followers

This is the layer furthest from you. Although they can follow you they will not have access to your profile, pictures or your name. They will only be able to see posts that you make available for everyone.
